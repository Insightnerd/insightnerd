About InsightNerd

InsightNerd is a technology publication built for people who want to understand and work with modern technology — not just read about it.

We cover:

  • Artificial Intelligence — news, tools, comparisons, and hands-on guides
  • Coding & Programming — languages, frameworks, and best practices
  • Data Analytics — SQL, Python, visualization, and data engineering
  • Tutorials & How-To Guides — step-by-step projects you can build
  • Software & Tools — reviews and comparisons of useful tools

Every article is written from real experience. We test the tools, write the code, and share what actually works — no fluff, no affiliate padding.
